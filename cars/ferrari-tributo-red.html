F8 Tributo

The F8 Tributo’s clean, muscular lines reflect state-of-the-art aerodynamic innovations developed in GT racing, making it an ideal model to display further weight-saving, performance-enhancing options from the Ferrari Genuine Accessories range. They combine to give the car an even more aggressive, sporting demeanour. These exclusive components include carbon fibre 20” wheels, side air intake splitter, kickplate and front spoiler, plus titanium exhaust tailpipes.
